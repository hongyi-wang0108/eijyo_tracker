## English

### Overview
Eijyo Tracker ("the app") is a tool that helps foreign residents in Japan track their permanent-residency (永住) application progress, organize required documents, and view explainable estimates of review time based on publicly available statistics. We take your privacy seriously. **The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.**

### Information we collect
**None.** We do not collect any personally identifiable information. The app has no user accounts, no login, and no server backend that receives your data.

### How your data is stored
All information you enter (such as nickname, application status, dates, document checklist, and supplement records) is stored **only on your device**, in the app's local database (Room) and local preferences (DataStore). This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it yourself (see below).

### Network access
The app requests the `INTERNET` permission for a single purpose: to **download public statistical data** (immigration processing statistics) from a public content-delivery network (jsDelivr / GitHub). This is a one-way download of public data. **No personal data is ever uploaded.** If the network is unavailable, the app falls back to cached or bundled public data and continues to work.

### Third-party services
The app contains **no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, and no crash-reporting SDKs**. We do not use any third-party tracking. The only external endpoint contacted is the public CDN used to fetch the public statistics file described above.

### Data export and deletion
- **Export:** You may, at your own initiative, export a JSON backup of your data to a location you choose via the system file picker. This file is created and controlled entirely by you.
- **Deletion:** You can permanently delete all local data at any time from Settings → "Delete all data", which clears the local database and preferences and returns the app to its first-run state. Uninstalling the app also removes all local data.

### Permissions
- **INTERNET** — only to download the public statistics file. Not used to transmit any personal data.

### Children's privacy
The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

### Changes to this policy
We may update this policy as the app evolves. The latest version will always be available at this URL, with an updated effective date.

本アプリには**広告SDK・分析SDK・クラッシュ報告SDKは一切含まれていません。** 第三者によるトラッキングは使用していません。外部に接続するのは、上記の公開統計ファイルを取得する公開CDNのみです。
